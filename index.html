I am an MSA graduate from the University of Illinois Urbana–Champaign. My research focuses on: (1) safe automated deployment of business strategies with AI; (2) agentic AI as business assistants for efficient and effective business analysis and decision support; and (3) pure NLP aimed at advancing the capabilities and robustness of current AI models.
